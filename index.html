Our Services

We specialize in providing comprehensive orthopaedic and surgical solutions, including mobility care products, orthopaedic supports and braces, trauma and surgical instruments, physiotherapy equipment, and electrotherapy devices. Our commitment to quality and reliability ensures that healthcare providers across Sri Lanka have access to the best medical equipment when they need it most.

About Us

Orthopaedic Surgery and Sports Medicine (Pvt) Ltd was incorporated in 2018 (ROC No: PV00201878)

The company stands as a leading distributor of orthopaedic, trauma, and sports medicine equipment in Sri Lanka. Our focus is to ensure accessibility to reliable, high-quality products, enabling healthcare professionals to deliver effective patient outcomes.

We provide dedicated 24/7 supply services for trauma and sports medicine instruments, physiotherapy and rehabilitation products, surgical power tools, and specialized solutions for OMF, craniotomy, and neurosurgery fields.
